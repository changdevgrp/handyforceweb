About Handy-Force

Your Home. Our Mission.

Handy-Force operates under Chang Development Group LLC and was built to deliver dependable, detail-focused handyman services with a customer-first mindset. From quick fixes to custom home upgrades, we treat every project like a mission: planned carefully, executed cleanly, and completed with pride.

Founded in Miami in 2017 and now proudly based in Saint Augustine, Handy-Force brings Florida roots, community care, and premium service standards to every job.
